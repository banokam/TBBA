Hello I am Bryant Anokam, a math tutor with a B.S. in Electrical Engineer and over 5 years of math tutoring experience. I have effectively worked with students of many different levels in private
one-on-one and group sessions and also in classroom settings. As a tutor, I pride myself on being a passionate instructor, patient and adaptive to the students’ needs, pacing myself thoroughly, breaking down complex problems and ideas with detailed step-by-step instruction, and giving constructive feedback to improve students analysis and solving skills.

I believe that every student has unlimited potential. My aim is to bring out each student’s full potential by giving them all the skills and knowledge that's needed to succeed. I want to instill students with the confidence and proficiency in math wherein it becomes easy and enjoyable to do. I truly enjoy giving back to my community with my knowledge of math. Knowing that I have made an impact in someone’s life and have undeniably changed their future for the better is all the joy that brings me back to tutoring. Tutoring isn’t a job for me but a passion I love.
